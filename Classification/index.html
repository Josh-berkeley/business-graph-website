18-Dimensional Taxonomy

Move beyond SIC codes to understand how companies actually make money. Our dynamic classification evolves with business model changes, providing mapping across multiple dimensions simultaneously.

Business Model

Revenue generation patterns, customer acquisition, value delivery mechanisms

How We Analyze:

  • Revenue Flow Analysis: Map primary, secondary, and emerging revenue streams
  • Value Chain Mapping: Identify where value is created, captured, and delivered
  • Customer Journey Analysis: Track acquisition, conversion, and retention patterns
  • Unit Economics Modeling: Analyze CAC, LTV, and gross margin structures
Example: Meridian Software classified as "Vertical SaaS + Marketplace Elements" for logistics automation - not just "Supply Chain Software"

Customer Segments

Target markets, customer types, enterprise vs consumer focus

How We Analyze:

  • Customer Size Analysis: SMB, Mid-market, Enterprise segmentation with ACV ranges
  • Vertical Concentration: Industry focus patterns and diversification metrics
  • Buying Behavior Mapping: Decision-making processes, buying cycles, stakeholder analysis
  • Multi-sided Market Detection: Platform dynamics and network effects identification
Example: Atlas Insights serves "Mid-market PE firms + Growth-stage companies" with different analytics value propositions

Revenue Patterns

Subscription, transaction, licensing, marketplace, hybrid models

How We Analyze:

  • Revenue Model Decomposition: Primary vs secondary revenue streams with % breakdown
  • Pricing Strategy Analysis: Usage-based, tiered, freemium, enterprise patterns
  • Revenue Recognition Patterns: Timing, duration, and predictability assessment
  • Monetization Evolution: How revenue models change with company maturity
Example: Nexus Analytics = "SaaS subscription (70%) + Usage-based (20%) + Professional services (10%)"

Competitive Dynamics

Market positioning, differentiation strategy, competitive moats

How We Analyze:

  • Competitive Set Mapping: Direct, indirect, and substitute competition identification
  • Differentiation Analysis: Product, pricing, distribution, and brand positioning
  • Moat Assessment: Network effects, switching costs, data advantages, regulatory barriers
  • Market Share Evolution: Historical and projected competitive positioning
Example: Meridian Software competes with "Legacy ERP modules + Point solutions + Horizontal platforms"

Technology Stack

Core technologies, infrastructure, innovation approach

How We Analyze:

  • Architecture Assessment: Cloud-native, hybrid, on-premise technology choices
  • Innovation Velocity: R&D investment, patent portfolio, technical team composition
  • Platform Strategy: API-first, developer tools, ecosystem enablement approach
  • Technical Debt Analysis: Legacy dependencies, modernization requirements
Example: Catalyst Analytics = "Cloud-native + API-first architecture + ML-powered insights"

Market Approach

Geographic focus, market entry strategy, expansion patterns

How We Analyze:

  • Geographic Revenue Distribution: US, EMEA, APAC breakdown with growth rates
  • Market Entry Strategy: Direct sales, partnerships, acquisitions, organic expansion
  • Localization Requirements: Product, legal, regulatory adaptation needs
  • Expansion Sequence: Historical and planned geographic rollout patterns
Example: Foundation Partners = "Direct sales + Channel partnerships + Geographic expansion"

Classification APIs

Integrate our 18-dimensional classification system directly into your investment workflows with real-time updates and flexible querying capabilities.

GET /classify/{company_id}

Classify any private company across all 18 dimensions with confidence scores and evidence sources.

{ "company_id": "meridian_software", "classification": { "business_model": "platform_saas", "customer_segments": ["enterprise", "smb"], "revenue_patterns": ["subscription", "usage"], "confidence": 0.94 } }
POST /universe/generate

Generate investment universes based on multi-dimensional criteria with precision filtering.

{ "criteria": { "business_model": ["b2b_saas"], "customer_segments": ["enterprise"], "revenue_patterns": ["subscription"] }, "universe_size": 2847 }
GET /peers/{company_id}

Discover business model peers with similarity scoring across multiple dimensions.
